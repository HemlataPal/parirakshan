Nature Conservation:

Objective: Our intent is to promote Organic farming by practicing it on our Farm and also motivate other farmers in the area to the same.



# Our Initiative! How You can Support? Information
1 Doing it yourself: We believe until we practice ourselves we cannot expect others to follow. • You can Fund Us: We need you to join and support the cause, however, if you are not physically available you can fund a running project. • You may browse to Whats going on click to see the details
2 Partner a farmer: Our fellow Farmers are interested in adapting organic methods of farming, however, due to lack of initial investment and low infrastructural support they are unable to. • You can partner with our farmers in more than one way e.g. Innovation, Technology, Funds etc. • You can write to us at projectsinfo@parirakshan.org with 'Partner a farmer' as the subject of the email
3 Buyback organic produce: Our farmers are mostly worried about their produce being bought at reasonable cost. • You can assure farmers to buy their produce in advance: It is a financial assurance and a motivation to continue farming • You can write to us at projectsinfo@parirakshan.org with 'Buyback organic produce' as the subject of the email

You already know that Organic farming methods protects environment, minimize soil degradation and erosion, decrease pollution, optimize biological productivity and promote a sound state of health.

We are a group of people from all walks of life who have developed a passion for sustainable farming practices. Our aim is to make sure that everyone benefits from a noble initiative such as organic farming that is good for the entire ecosystem including us humans who can consume pesticide free produce.

Many farmers are inclined to sell their land and move to cities because of countless difficulties faced by them. We encourage farmers to try sustainable farming initiatives and couple them with revenue generating activities such as village tourism. This provides employment to village youth and builds a solid foundation for each village.
