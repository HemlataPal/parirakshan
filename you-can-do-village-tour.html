If you're looking to bring about change in the lives of others, then village tourism is a great way. By volunteering a vacation you spend time with Nature and help us create employment opportunities for locals. It will also prevent migration of locals to cities. Migration of locals from their native place pose a threat to environment as they have been not just the habitants but also have played a key role in the preservation with their age old nature friendly lifestyle.

Age group migrating population is mostly between 16 â€“ 40 years, forcing to leave their elders, children and wives behind. Major impact is seen on the Land as the other family members usually does not engage in agriculture as much. As a result of which, land is becoming barren and our rich cultural heritage of hills is dying slowly.
