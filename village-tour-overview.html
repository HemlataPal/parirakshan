Village Tourism

People in Hills have been living in harmony with Nature for generations. They have had a fulfilling, healthy life while people in cities always wondered why! We do not want that culture to die in the race of development but grow along.

For ages, communities in Hills have been united by shared ecological, social-economic and cultural-spiritual values. They have practiced age old alternatives to ecologically destructive system of to fulfilling community needs with minimal ecological impact.

We believe their ways can sustain our growing needs as it sustained there for generations! Through Village Tourism we create an opportunity for you to know their ways, stay with them and learn the mantra to holistic living! Come alone or come in a group, community will embrace you the way it embraced us. Spending time in a village and understanding how it functions, can go a long way in building an individual's knowledge and sensitivity towards important issues.

If you're looking to bring about change in the lives of others, then village tourism is a great way to achieve it first hand while living amongst them. By choosing a volunteer vacation in a village, all the funds you spend will be directed towards the development needs of the village and will help create sustainable employment opportunities for them. It will also prevent migration of workers to cities far away from their home, in the search of an income.

We usually take batches of 8-10 persons at a time. Don't worry, we've got all the basics covered. Contact us and our team will revert to you! Depending on your availability, we offer multiple options to choose from and participate. Select how you wish to Act and we will arrange it for you!

Promote local culture and sensitization

# Category Name of the Project Project Detail
6 Village Tourism Promote local culture and sensitization Problem statement:

People in Hills have been living in harmony with Nature for generations. They have had a fulfilling, healthy life however, their practices are dying in the race of development and influence from the urban ways of living.

Solution:

We need to promote and support their practices. Acknowledge the benefits of traditional ways of living. Reverse the learning process from rural to urban areas. Therefore village tourism will create a platform for spreading these social and cultural values.

Scope of work for you/ how you can support?

1. Volunteer your time in taking up a social project or research on the way of life in hills
2. Know about the local culture, products and motivate them to maintain their culture instead of migrating
3. Fund our initiative of social welfare and development – arrange for medical facilities
4. Motivate your friends and neighbors to volunteer too and plan a some time off

How Parirakshan will support?

1. Project management
2. Boarding and lodging, including travel
3. Logistical arrangements and local support

Location:

Neri village, Uttarakhand and expand it further in neighboring villages too
