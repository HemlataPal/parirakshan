Social Welfare

In the race of fulfilling the ever ending demands, we are neglecting the fundamental fact that all Natural resources are in abundance but perishable. Nature is the source to our life; we are destroying the source with our casual approach towards it and the people who have been maintaining the balance between consumption and preservation. This cannot go for ever. This phenomenon is affecting the people in Hills as well and they are pressured to either quit or adopt unsustainable methods.

The Government of India has many programs in place for the development of villages. However, the success of such programs hinges on the interest shown by citizens. We need to lend a helping hand to bring important messages to the notice of as many people as possible.

Social welfare projects in the small communities of hills are of utmost importance as they focus on equipping them with the knowledge & techniques required to sustain the pressures of modern world. We focus at topics such as primary education, skill development, legal awareness and etc.

Educate a child, transform society

# Category Name of the Project Project Detail
5 Social welfare and development Educate a child, transform society Problem statement:

Literacy is a key for socio-economic progress. Many children, are relegated to small futures by virtue of their race, income or gender. Poverty in India has deterred the pursuit of formal education as education does not get highest priority among the poor as compared to other basic necessities.

Solution:

Parirakshan Organica Foundation, aims to identify, enroll and ensure completion of a quality education for children and make education possible for them. Also, facilitate children in their learning and development by home schooling

Scope of work for you/ how you can support?

1. Fund children education, health and general well-being
2. Donate clothes (summer and winter) and toys
3. Donate books, copies and stationary as per their need

How Parirakshan will support?

1. Project management
2. Arrange home schooling facility by identifying mainstream tutors engaged on subsidized tuition fee basis
      a. Arrange Learning materials and stationary
3. Arrange extra-curricular activities
      a. Fun activities such as games, spend time at natural sites etc.
      b. Experiential learning educational tours, visit to museums, etc.
4. Arrange for medical attention and healthy/ nutritional meals

Location:

Noida, Ghaziabad and Neri, Uttarakhand
