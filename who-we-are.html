Who We Are

Parirakshan Organica is a 'not for profit' organization, registered under 'Pursuant to sub-section(2) of section 7 of the Companies Act, 2013 and rule 8 of the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014'

The effect of destruction is reaching hills at very fast pace and therefore we decided to hold our grounds in the Hills of Uttarakhand (to start with) and preserve what tomorrow would also become a barren land.

We realised it long back and denied to become a part of this process of destruction rather decided to take the initiative of doing something about the continuous damage we are causing. Only complaining how the temperature of Delhi increased in last few years and how there is no one to clean river will not help. We also know that, like us, there are many more people who want to contribute and only when this number of Doers increases that the Change will come.

We decided to hold our grounds (Hills of Uttarakhand) here and preserve what tomorrow would also become a barren land. While we were actively working for the cause at individual level, July 24, 2014, Parirakshan Organica was officially launched. We are sure that through this initiative many more like-minded individuals, groups and organizations will join us and volunteer in administrating innovative solutions to the threat posed to our Bio-diversity and the fundamental problems faced by our people in Hills. We warmly welcome anyone who has time and energy to make Hills their home and to tell us how to do this better!

We have some very innovative ways of collaboration, if you wish to contribute and also earn, please goto: What can You do section or reach us at info@parirakshan.com for more details.

Let us come together, Lets Act!
