How it Works

Organic Farming:

We are a group of people from all walks of life who have developed a passion for sustainable farming practices. Our aim is to make sure that everyone benefits from a noble initiative such as organic farming that is good for the entire ecosystem including us humans who can consume pesticide free produce.

Many farmers are inclined to sell their land and move to cities because of countless difficulties faced by them. We encourage farmers to try sustainable farming initiatives and couple them with revenue generating activities such as village tourism. This provides employment to village youth and builds a solid foundation for each village.

Village Tourism:

If you're looking to bring about change in the lives of others, then village tourism is a great way to achieve it first hand while living amongst them. By choosing a volunteer vacation in a village, all the funds you spend will be directed towards the development needs of the village and will help create sustainable employment opportunities for them. It will also prevent migration of workers to cities far away from their home, in the search of an income.

Village tourism is an upcoming concept for those who want to a change of scenery while making a difference in the lives of others. Such holidays are already popular globally and are known as volunteer vacations.
